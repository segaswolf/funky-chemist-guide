Synthesize these first and stock in jugs — they aren't available directly from the dispenser. Water comes from the sink, treat it as free.

Physical damage. T1 covers general Brute. T2 targets specific subtypes. Recommended mixes at the bottom combine coverage for typical scenarios.

Thermal damage — Heat, Cold, Shock. T1 covers all three. T2 adds scaling or specialization. Burn does NOT cause bleeding, so no need to pair with Saline.

Toxin damage and bloodstream contamination. Multiver is always useful. Stellibinin is the go-to for plant/mushroom poisons (Amatoxin).

Radiation damage. Always pair Arithrazine with Libital — it causes Brute damage as a side effect.

Airloss, asphyxiation, and blood loss. Saline restores blood volume — always pair with anti-bleed meds for hemorrhage. Epinephrine only activates in Critical.
